About Us

At Maes Landscaping LLC, we are a family-owned company dedicated to transforming outdoor spaces with expertise, creativity, and a commitment to sustainability. With over 20 years of experience, our team brings deep industry knowledge and a passion for landscape design. Rooted in the community we proudly serve, our mission is to create beautiful, functional, and eco-friendly landscapes that enhance both residential and commercial properties. We prioritize sustainable landscaping solutions that conserve resources, promote biodiversity, and contribute to a healthier environment. Beyond landscaping, we are committed to improving our community—one project at a time. Through quality craftsmanship, personalized service, and innovative design, we strive to enrich the lives of our clients and create outdoor spaces that inspire. At Maes Landscaping LLC, we don’t just design landscapes—we cultivate beauty, sustainability, and community.
